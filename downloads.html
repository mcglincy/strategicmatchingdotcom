Download Instructions

Obtain a serial number and password from Strategic Matching. Enter the serial number in the search box and click on the Search Downloads button. A list of available downloads will be displayed in a new window. Two or more downloads might be listed:  one to replace your current installation (if any) with a full installation of the latest version and one to upgrade your current installation to the latest version. Click on the link to the download of your choice.

All downloads are SetupBuilder 10 (c) 2020 Lindersoft setup or update programs signed by Strategic Matching.

You will prompted for the password when you run a full installation setup program.
